# Means-ends analysis

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
A systems-analysis method that connects higher-level goals to subordinate means so analysts can clarify how actions are expected to contribute to desired ends.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 03 - Systems Analysis]]

## Core ideas
- Separate ends from the means used to pursue them.
- Make implicit assumptions about contribution chains explicit.
- Use the structure to compare alternatives and identify missing links.
