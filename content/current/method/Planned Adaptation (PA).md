# Planned Adaptation (PA)

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
A conceptual approach to adaptation that focuses on the institutional rules, triggers, and evaluative capacities needed to revise policy over time. It helps connect DMDU methods to adaptive governance and implementation.

## Downward links
- [[DMDU Ch 13 - A Conceptual Model of Planned Adaptation (PA)]]
- [[DMDU Ch 14 - DMDU into Practice - Adaptive Delta Management in The Netherlands]]

## Core ideas
- Separate primary rules from the secondary rules that revise them.
- Identify triggers, events, and evaluative capacities.
- Compare ad hoc, planned, and systematic adaptation arrangements.
- Treat adaptation as an organizational capability, not just an analytic output.
