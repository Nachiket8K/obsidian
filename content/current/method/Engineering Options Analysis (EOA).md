# Engineering Options Analysis (EOA)

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
A method for valuing flexibility in infrastructure and engineering decisions under deep uncertainty. EOA focuses on staged choices, optionality, and the range of outcomes across plausible futures.

## Downward links
- [[DMDU Ch 06 - Engineering Options Analysis (EOA)]]
- [[DMDU Ch 11 - Engineering Options Analysis (EOA) - Applications]]

## Core ideas
- Treat flexibility as a design asset, not a residual feature.
- Compare fixed, reactive, and proactive strategies across scenarios.
- Use option logic without forcing every question into a financial market frame.
- Support staged investment and adaptable system design.
