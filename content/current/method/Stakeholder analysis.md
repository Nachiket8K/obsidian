# Stakeholder analysis

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
A practical actor-analysis method for identifying relevant stakeholders, their interests, influence, stakes, and likely positions regarding a policy problem.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 04 - Actor Analysis]]

## Core ideas
- Identify who matters to the problem and why.
- Compare influence, dependence, interests, and perceptions across stakeholders.
- Use the result to anticipate support, resistance, and negotiation needs.
