# Open exploration

## Upward links
- [[EMA Workbench Documentation - Source]]

## Summary
A sampling-based exploratory modeling method that evaluates many scenarios, policies, or both in order to reveal vulnerabilities, trade-offs, and broad behavioural patterns across uncertain futures.

## Core ideas
- Sample uncertainty space and decision space broadly instead of optimizing immediately.
- Treat scenarios, policies, and experiments as structured inputs to a larger decision-support workflow.
- Use exploratory runs to surface failure regimes before narrowing attention to candidate strategies.
- Separate experiment generation from later analysis so results can be revisited and reinterpreted.
