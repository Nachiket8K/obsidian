# Feature scoring

## Upward links
- [[EMA Workbench Documentation - Source]]

## Summary
An analysis method that ranks inputs by how strongly they influence outcomes or classifications, helping analysts focus on the uncertainties or levers most relevant to a decision problem.

## Core ideas
- Rank uncertainties and levers by their explanatory importance rather than inspecting everything equally.
- Support both regression-style and classification-style interpretations.
- Help narrow attention before more detailed scenario discovery or robustness analysis.
- Work well as a lightweight complement to more formal global sensitivity analysis.
