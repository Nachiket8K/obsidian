- The pickup and dropoff points for each workstation are indicated using the letters P and D respectively. Note that stations 5 and 6 share these points.
- Table 17-1 gives the average number of material moves between workstations per 16 hour day. This information forms the distribution of pickup point to dropoff point AGV movements. Each individual movement can be determin
- A material move requires an AGV to move from it current location to the pickup point and then from the pickup point to the dropoff point. Each AGV moves at the rate of 5 feet per second and takes 30 seconds for each drop
