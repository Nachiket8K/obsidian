## Summary
This chapter discusses a beyond lean analysis of the operation of a single workstation, both with and without operations detractors: breakdowns, part reworking, as well as setup and batching. An analytic model is used to compute the workstation utilization as well as the average time and number of parts in the buffer of the workstation for the case of no detractors.
