# Research methods

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Research methods are the practical approaches chosen to answer research questions and close decision-relevant knowledge gaps.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 07 - The Research Plan]]

## Key points
- Method choice should follow from the question, not the other way around.
- Feasibility, timing, and decision relevance matter alongside rigor.
- Policy analysis often requires combining multiple methods.
