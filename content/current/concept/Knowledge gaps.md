# Knowledge gaps

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Knowledge gaps are the unresolved questions, missing evidence, or uncertain relationships that prevent a problem analysis from supporting action with sufficient confidence.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 06 - From Synthesis to Plan of Action]]
- [[PAMS Ch 07 - The Research Plan]]

## Key points
- They emerge when synthesis reveals what is still unknown or disputed.
- They help transform broad uncertainty into targeted follow-on analysis.
- They are a bridge from diagnosis to research planning.
