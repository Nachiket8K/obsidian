# Adaptive planning

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
Adaptive planning designs a current plan together with contingent adjustments that can be activated later. In the book it acts as a bridge between method design, implementation, and governance.

## Key points
- Preserve flexibility without deferring all action.
- Connect monitoring information to predefined responses.
- Sequence actions so future choices remain open where possible.
