# Interdependencies

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Interdependencies are the mutual dependencies among actors, decisions, or system elements that make outcomes contingent on coordination, negotiation, or adaptation.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 04 - Actor Analysis]]

## Key points
- No actor can act as if others are irrelevant.
- Dependence may involve resources, information, timing, legitimacy, or implementation capacity.
- Interdependencies are a main source of policy complexity.
