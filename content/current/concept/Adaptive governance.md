# Adaptive governance

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
Adaptive governance is the organizational and institutional capacity to monitor, evaluate, and revise policies as conditions change. In the DMDU book it connects formal methods to implementation practice.

## Key points
- Requires authority, monitoring capability, and processes for revision.
- Depends on how tightly analytic insights are coupled to decision institutions.
- Becomes critical when policies are long-term, contested, and high-stakes.
