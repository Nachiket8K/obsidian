# Rich problem description

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
A rich problem description synthesizes system, actor, and future analyses into a fuller account of what the problem is, why it is contested, and what makes it difficult to address.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 06 - From Synthesis to Plan of Action]]

## Key points
- It integrates partial analyses rather than keeping them separate.
- It captures technical, social, and institutional dimensions together.
- It provides the basis for framing, action proposals, and research follow-up.
