# Problem formulation

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Problem formulation defines the problem, its boundaries, its relevant dimensions, and the possible points of intervention before detailed analysis or design proceeds.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 02 - Problem Formulation in Complex Environments]]

## Key points
- Good formulation reduces the risk of solving the wrong problem.
- Complex policy problems combine technical, social, institutional, and normative dimensions.
- Formulation is iterative and shaped by the policy process rather than fixed in advance.
