# Monitor-and-adapt

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
A core DMDU stance that replaces one-shot prediction-centred planning with iterative monitoring, learning, and adjustment. It is the practical logic shared across adaptive planning methods in the book.

## Key points
- Accept that plans will need revision as conditions evolve.
- Design monitoring and action thresholds into policy from the start.
- Treat learning as part of implementation, not a separate phase.
