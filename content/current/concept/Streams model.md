# Streams model

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
The streams model explains policy change through the temporary coupling of problem, policy, and politics streams, often at moments when policy windows open.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 02 - Problem Formulation in Complex Environments]]

## Key points
- Problems, solutions, and politics develop partly independently.
- Policy windows create temporary opportunities for coupling them.
- The model helps explain why timing and entrepreneurship matter in policy processes.
