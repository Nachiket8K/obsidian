# Causal loop diagrams

## Upward links
- [[The Delft Method for System Dynamics - Source]]

## Summary
Causal Loop Diagrams are qualitative SD diagrams used to communicate causal structure and identify feedback loops. They are especially valuable during conceptualisation and explanation.

## Downward links
- [[Delft SD Ch 02 - The first modelling cycle]]
- [[Delft SD Ch 06 - Problem Formulation and Conceptualisation]]

## Key points
- Highlight reinforcing and balancing loops.
- Support communication before full quantification.
- Help connect behaviour narratives to structure.
