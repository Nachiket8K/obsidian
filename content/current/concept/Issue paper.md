# Issue paper

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
An issue paper is a short, structured policy-analysis document that presents a problem diagnosis, storyline, and practical direction for action to a client or decision-maker.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 08 - Preparing an Issue Paper]]
- [[PAMS Annex - Storylines for Framed Problems]]

## Key points
- It distills analysis into a form usable by decision-makers.
- It depends on framing choices and narrative structure as well as evidence.
- It is meant to support action, not just summarize research.
