# Geospatial knowledge graphs

## Upward links
- [[GIS&T Body of Knowledge - Source]]

## Summary
Geospatial knowledge graphs organize geographic entities, events, and relationships in graph structures that support integration, retrieval, and semantic reasoning. In this vault they are especially relevant because your note system itself is being designed as a semantically linked knowledge graph.

## Key points
- GeoKGs support data integration through shared concepts, typed relationships, and reusable identifiers.
- They help connect geospatial with non-geospatial knowledge across domains.
- They are relevant both as a GIScience topic and as a design analogy for how this vault is being structured.
