## Summary
Data pipelines in Python refer to chained workflows for reading, transforming, joining, analyzing, and exporting data. In this vault they connect general-purpose tabular analysis with geospatial processing, simulation outputs, and decision-support workflows.

## Key points
- Pipelines help turn raw source data into structured analytical inputs and interpretable outputs.
- pandas often handles the tabular parts, while GeoPandas adds spatial capabilities where geometry matters.
- Modeling and simulation workflows benefit from explicit, reusable pipelines because they improve reproducibility and traceability.
