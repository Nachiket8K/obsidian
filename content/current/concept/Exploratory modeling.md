# Exploratory modeling

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
Exploratory modeling uses repeated model runs over wide ranges of assumptions to reveal vulnerabilities, trade-offs, and robust responses. It is one of the central computational ideas behind the DMDU family.

## Key points
- Use models to explore assumption spaces, not just forecast outcomes.
- Supports stress testing across diverse futures and worldviews.
- Creates the evidence base for scenario discovery and robustness analysis.
