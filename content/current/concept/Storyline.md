# Storyline

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
A storyline is the narrative structure that organizes a diagnosis, highlights a frame, and makes an issue paper intelligible and persuasive to its audience.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 08 - Preparing an Issue Paper]]
- [[PAMS Annex - Storylines for Framed Problems]]

## Key points
- Storylines shape how a problem and its implications are understood.
- They connect evidence, framing, and proposed action into a coherent whole.
- Different diagnoses often require different narrative structures.
