# Climate stress test

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
A climate stress test explores how system performance changes across a wide plausible range of climate conditions. In Decision Scaling it is the central step for revealing climate-sensitive vulnerabilities before using formal climate projections.

## Downward links
- [[DMDU Ch 12 - Decision Scaling (DS) - Decision Support for Climate Change]]

## Key points
- Focuses on what the system can tolerate.
- Reduces dependence on any single projection set.
- Improves the fit between climate information and decision needs.
