# Plan of action

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
A plan of action translates problem synthesis into proposed next steps, responsibilities, and lines of follow-on activity that are feasible within the policy setting.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 06 - From Synthesis to Plan of Action]]
- [[PAMS Ch 08 - Preparing an Issue Paper]]

## Key points
- It moves from diagnosis toward action without pretending uncertainty has disappeared.
- It usually depends on framing choices and identified knowledge gaps.
- It can include analytic, organizational, or participatory follow-up steps.
