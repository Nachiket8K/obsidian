# Adaptive policies

## Upward links
- [[The Delft Method for System Dynamics - Source]]

## Summary
Adaptive policies explicitly connect policy levers to monitored system conditions, allowing policy settings to change as the system evolves. In this book they are contrasted with static or open-loop policies.

## Downward links
- [[Delft SD Ch 09 - Model use and policy testing]]

## Key points
- Adaptive policies add explicit feedback between performance and intervention.
- They can be clearer and more stable than ad hoc policy change.
- Their value is strongest when conditions evolve and robustness matters.
