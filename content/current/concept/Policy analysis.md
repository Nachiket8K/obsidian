# Policy analysis

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Policy analysis is the disciplined support of policy processes through problem diagnosis, structuring, comparison of alternatives, and advice for action. In this vault it is the main bridge from multi-actor public problems to reusable decision-support methods.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 01 - Introduction]]
- [[PAMS Ch 08 - Preparing an Issue Paper]]

## Key points
- Supports action in contested settings rather than assuming one agreed objective.
- Connects analytic work to clients, stakeholders, and policy processes.
- Often begins with structuring the problem before evaluating solutions.
