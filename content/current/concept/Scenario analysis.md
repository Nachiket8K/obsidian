# Scenario analysis

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Scenario analysis compares alternative plausible futures so analysts can reason about uncertainty, contextual change, and the robustness of proposed actions.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 05 - Exploring the Future]]

## Key points
- It focuses on plausible contextual differences rather than one forecast.
- It supports reflection on risks, opportunities, and policy sensitivity.
- It is useful when uncertainty is structural, contested, or long-term.
