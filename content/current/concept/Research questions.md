# Research questions

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Research questions translate broad knowledge gaps into targeted lines of inquiry that can guide method selection, evidence gathering, and follow-on analysis.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 07 - The Research Plan]]

## Key points
- Good questions narrow uncertainty into manageable inquiry.
- They should be linked to decision relevance rather than curiosity alone.
- They provide the bridge from diagnosis to research design.
