# Wicked problems

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Wicked problems are ill-structured problems with contested values, many actors, unstable boundaries, and no single objectively best solution.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 02 - Problem Formulation in Complex Environments]]

## Key points
- The problem cannot be fully separated from disagreements about its definition.
- Solutions often create new problems or redistribute burdens.
- Analysis must support learning, negotiation, and reframing rather than simple optimization.
