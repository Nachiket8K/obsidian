# Policy cycle

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
The policy cycle is a stylized representation of recurring policy subprocesses such as agenda setting, analysis, decision-making, implementation, and evaluation.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 01 - Introduction]]

## Key points
- It offers a simple way to locate where analysis is being applied.
- Real policy processes are usually messier than the cycle suggests.
- It remains useful as a heuristic even when not descriptively complete.
