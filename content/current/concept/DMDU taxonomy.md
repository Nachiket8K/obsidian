# DMDU taxonomy

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]

## Summary
The DMDU taxonomy organizes approaches and tools by policy architecture, scenario generation, robustness metrics, and vulnerability analysis. It is the best synthesis note for comparing methods across the book.

## Downward links
- [[DMDU Ch 15 - Supporting DMDU - A Taxonomy of Approaches and Tools]]

## Key points
- Acts as a crosswalk between approaches rather than a new standalone method.
- Helps combine methods based on problem structure.
- Clarifies where methods differ: architecture, analysis, metrics, and implementation logic.
