# Actor analysis

## Upward links
- [[Policy Analysis of Multi-Actor Systems - Source]]

## Summary
Actor analysis studies the relevant actors in a policy problem, their interests, perceptions, resources, institutions, and relationships, so analysts can understand constraints and opportunities for action.

## Downward links
- [[PAMS Ch 04 - Actor Analysis]]

## Key points
- Problem owners rarely control the full system in multi-actor settings.
- Actor perceptions and values shape both the definition of the problem and the viability of solutions.
- Institutions and networks matter alongside technical content.
