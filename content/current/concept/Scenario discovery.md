# Scenario discovery

## Upward links
- [[DMDU - Source]]
- [[EMA Workbench Documentation - Source]]

## Summary
Scenario discovery is a way to identify the combinations of conditions under which a strategy fails or succeeds. In DMDU it helps explain model results and connect large ensembles of futures back to human reasoning.

## Key points
- Searches for decision-relevant futures instead of arbitrary scenarios.
- Supports communication about vulnerabilities and failure regimes.
- Pairs naturally with robustness analysis and exploratory modeling.
