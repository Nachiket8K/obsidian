# Brain

Welcome to the public knowledge graph for this Obsidian vault.

This site is organized as a **network of reusable notes** rather than a folder-first archive. The goal is to make concepts, methods, sources, and applications discoverable through links, backlinks, and the Quartz graph view.

## How to use this site

- Use the **graph view** to explore relationships across notes.
- Use the **search bar** to jump directly to concepts, methods, books, or case studies.
- Follow **backlinks** and **lateral links** to move across related ideas.
- Treat folders as implementation structure, but treat links as the main navigation layer.

## Knowledge graph structure

This vault follows four levels of knowledge:

1. **Sources** — books, documentation sets, and imported material
2. **Concepts** — reusable ideas and theoretical building blocks
3. **Methods** — analytical and modelling approaches
4. **Applications** — practical cases and domain examples
